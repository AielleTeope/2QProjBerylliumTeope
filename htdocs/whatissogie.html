What does the acronym SOGIE stand for?

SOGIE is an acronym for Sexual Orientation, Gender Identity, and Gender Expression. Everyone has an assigned SOGIE, and it isn't exclusive to only the memebers of the LGBTQ+ community. The concept of SOGIE introduces how nuanced a person can be, and how one aspect of their identity isn't inherently tied to another.

For example, someone may tend to dress in more feminine clothes like dresses and skirts, but that doesn't necessarily mean that they identify as a woman.

Each aspect of SOGIE is a spectrum, and it may even take others years to figure out where exactly they lie on that spectrum.

An easy way of explaining SOGIE is the genderbread person, illustrating the differences between the aspects.
