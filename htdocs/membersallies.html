Some well-known members and allies of the community

Geraldine Roman

  • first transgender person to be elected to the Congress of the Philippines
  • stepped into office on June 30, 2016
  • she is also first transgender military officer and reservist of the Armed Forces of the Philippines (Lieutenant Colonel)

Pia Wurtzbach

  • won Miss Universe 2015, being the first to earn the title after four decades
  • during her reign, she chose HIV/AIDS as the main focus of her advocacy
  • on May 3, 2017, she was appointed as a UNAIDS Goodwill Ambassador for Asia and the Pacific
